How To Pitch A Tent

  1. Go Outside(with your tent)
  2. Touch Grass
  3. Take your tent out of the bag and grab a tarp if one is not included
  4. Lay the tarp out and make sure it is big enough to cover the entire bottom of the tent
  5. Lay the tent over the tarp
  6. Put the poles together and stick them through the sleeves so that the tent stands upright
  7. Stake the tent to the ground
  8. Take the rainfly and put it over the tent(make sure the sides are lined up)
  9. Attach the rainfly to the tent(Attachements may very depending on the tent. Some can just be clipped onto the tent, while others may need to be tied onto the tent.)
  10. Zip open the tent, put all your stuff in, and you're ready to camp!
